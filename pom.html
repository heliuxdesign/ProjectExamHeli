Planned orbital missions

SpaceX Dragons are launched into orbit by Falcon vehicles. Dragon is a spacecraft, and it is capable to carry up to 7 passangers to and from Earth orbit. SpaceX has three different launch vehicles; Falcon 1, Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy. Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy are both operational.

The Falcon family`s typical missions are cargo delivery and crewed flights to the International Space Station, launch of communications satellites and Earth observation satellites to geostationary transfer orbits and low Earth orbits.
