The International Space Station (ISS) is a modular spacestation, in low Earth Orbit. It is a multinational collaborative project involving five participating space agencies: NASA (United States), Roscosmos (Russia), JAXA (Japan), ESA (Europe), and CSA (Canada). Purpose of ISS are scientific research, Exploration, and education and cultural outreach. Since 2012 SpaceX has been delivering cargo to and from the ISS. In 2020 SpaceX started to transport people to the Manned Orbiting Laboratory (MOL) under NASA`s commercial Crew Program. Read more from SpaceX.
